Reinventing the traditional pol house typology of Ahmedabad into temporal housing for student living. While maintaining traditional elements essential to the pol typology, the proposed housing reimagines the pol as a more flexible and open community dwelling. The block becomes a populated and vibrant community where residents interact with the surrounding informal economies.
